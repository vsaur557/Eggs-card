Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish perfect for any model.This classic omelette beaten edds cooked to perfection , optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables or meats.

Prepartion time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation : 5 minutes
  • Cooking : 5 Minutes

    • Ingradients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling : cheese, diced vegetables. cooked meats ,herbs

      • Instructions

    1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl beat the eggs with a pich of salt and paper unfil they are welln mixed .

    2. Heat the pan : Place a non- stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

    3. Cook the omelette : Once the butter is metted and bubbling ,pour in the egg . Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs envenly coat the surface.

    4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begins to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle , sprinkly your chosen fillings over one half of the omlettle.

    5. Fold and serve: As the omlette continues to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filling . Let it cook for another minute ,then slide it onto a plate.

    6. Enjoy : servehot,with additional salt and paper if needed.
